Rear Pew Mirror

A four player first person shooter where players can only shoot directly behind them. Players are assisted by a small mirror that shows the view behind them. The goal of the game is to take control of the objective by standing on it, and holding it for 90 seconds. Control movement with the left stick, shoot with RT, reload with X, jump with A, and use powerups with the left bumper.
